The Spear of Agni

An Original Indian Fantasy eBook

About The Story

A fantasy adventure set in ancient India featuring magical weapons, powerful warriors, and the journey of a young hero named Aarav.

Prologue

Raat ka andhera Vindhya ke jungle par chhaya hua tha. Ek purane mandir ke andar ek tapasvi ek divya spear ki raksha kar raha tha. Tabhi Rudraveer naam ka ek shaktishali yoddha us spear ko churaane aaya. Lekin spear achanak gaayab ho gaya.

Chapter 1 – Aarav Ka Safar

Aarav ek chhote gaon ka yuva tha jo ek lohar ka beta tha. Ek din jungle mein usse ek chamakta hua spear mila. Jaise hi usne spear ko chhua, uske haath par ek agni nishaan ubhar aaya.

Chapter 2 – Maya Ki Entry

Maya ek fearless archer thi jo Aarav ko batati hai ki woh Agni-Spear ka naya rakshak hai. Rudraveer poore Bharat mein spear ko dhoond raha hai.
